📝 CLASS NOTES — What is PMI Analysis?
PMI = Plus, Minus, Interesting — a structured thinking tool invented by Edward de Bono. It forces balanced analysis by looking at ALL sides of a design, not just likes and dislikes.
PLUS
What is good? Strengths?
MINUS
What are the downsides?
INTERESTING
What is unique or surprising?

📝 TEEL PARAGRAPH STRUCTURE
TEEL is a paragraph structure for research and analytical writing. Your paragraph should be 6–8 sentences long.

T Topic sentence

Introduce the main idea — name the restaurant and make a claim

E Evidence

Provide specific facts, details, or examples

E Explain

Explain HOW the evidence supports your topic sentence

L Link

Connect back to the topic or link to the next point
