↔ DIMENSION LINES — How to Add Them
1. Dimension lines sit outside the drawing, parallel to the edge being measured
2. Use thin lines with arrows or tick marks at each end
3. Measurement number goes centred above the line
4. Overall dimensions placed furthest from the drawing
5. Minimum 10 mm gap between outline and first dimension line
