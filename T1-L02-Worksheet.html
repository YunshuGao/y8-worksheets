What is Isometric Drawing?

Key fact: Isometric means equal measure (iso = equal, metric = measure). All three axes are equally spaced at 120° apart. Lines stay parallel — there are no vanishing points.

★ Support — Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps carefully with your isometric dot paper:

  • Step 1: Draw a vertical line through 6 dots (this is the height)
  • Step 2: From the bottom dot, draw a line going down-left following the dots (30°)
  • Step 3: From the bottom dot, draw a line going down-right following the dots (30°)
  • Step 4: Repeat from the top dot — two more 30° lines
  • Step 5: Add vertical lines from the ends of the bottom lines
  • Step 6: Connect remaining corners with parallel 30° lines
