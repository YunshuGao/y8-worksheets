🔁 KITCHEN WORKFLOW ZONES
The Golden Triangle of kitchen workflow: food moves in ONE direction from delivery to customer. No backtracking = less contamination and more efficiency.
Receive
Near door — deliveries, fridge, dry storage
Prepare
Centre — bench, HWB, cutting space
Cook
Near window — grill, exhaust fan
Serve
At window — serving counter
Wash
Near water — dish sink, drying
📏 FLOOR PLAN DRAWING RULES
Your floor plan must:
• Fit inside a 30 cm × 12 cm rectangle (1:20 scale)
• Use correct architectural symbols from Lesson 12
• Label every piece of equipment in neat capital letters
• Show workflow arrows indicating food flow direction
• Leave at least 3 cm aisle space between equipment (= 60 cm real)

Floor Plan Checklist

🟢 Support
  • Use the pre-drawn template with zones already marked
  • Cut out equipment shapes and arrange before drawing
  • Focus on 4 essential items: grill, sink, bench, fridge
🟣 Extension
  • Create a second alternative layout and compare
  • Calculate total usable floor area after subtracting equipment
  • Add detailed dimensions for every piece of equipment
