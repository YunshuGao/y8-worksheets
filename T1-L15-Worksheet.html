Model Construction — Cutting and Assembly

Safely cutting, scoring, folding and assembling the basic container box model

Learning Intention: We are learning to safely cut, score, fold, and assemble a 1:20 scale model of our container restaurant.
? DO NOW — Safety First

Write 4 safety rules for using cutting tools in the workshop.

⚠ SAFETY RULES — NON-NEGOTIABLE
These rules are NON-NEGOTIABLE. Breaking any rule = immediate loss of tool privileges for the lesson.
Safety mat: ALWAYS cut on a safety mat — never on the desk
Cut away: Blade moves AWAY from your body
Retract blades: Retract craft knife blade when NOT cutting
Metal ruler: Use METAL ruler for knife cutting — never plastic
Scissors: Carry point-DOWN, close when not in use
Report injuries: Report ALL injuries immediately

I have read and understand the safety rules:

🛠 CONSTRUCTION STEPS — Follow This Order
Cut outer edges — Cut along all SOLID outer lines of the net
Cut serving window — Cut the window opening on the FRONT face
Score fold lines — Score ALL dashed lines — press firmly but do NOT cut through
Fold — Fold inward along scored lines — pencil marks go INSIDE
Dry fit — Hold the box together WITHOUT glue to check alignment
Glue tabs — One tab at a time, tape to hold while PVA dries
Key difference: SCORING creates a groove for folding (press firmly but do NOT cut through). CUTTING goes all the way through the cardboard. If you cut a fold line by accident, repair with masking tape on the inside.
