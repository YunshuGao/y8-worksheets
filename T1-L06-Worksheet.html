🏠 ZONES IN A COMMERCIAL KITCHEN
Cooking Zone
Grill, fryer, hotplate, oven
Preparation Zone
Benches, cutting boards, assembly area
Storage Zone
Fridge (<5°C), freezer, dry goods shelves
Wash-up Zone
Dish sinks + SEPARATE hand wash basin
Service Zone
Counter, POS/register, menu display
Utilities
Power, water in/out, waste, ventilation
Key constraint: Your container is only 2.44 m wide. These zones must be arranged in a linear sequence along the length!
