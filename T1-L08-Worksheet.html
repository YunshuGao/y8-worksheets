⚠ COMMON ISOMETRIC ERRORS TO AVOID
45° instead of 30°
Most common error! Use dot paper or set square to check.
Leaning Verticals
Vertical lines MUST be perfectly vertical — use a ruler.
Wrong Face Angle
Features must follow the angle of the face they sit on.
Incorrect Proportions
Container is 2.5× longer than it is wide — check ratio.
Freehand Lines
Use a ruler for all straight lines — freehand looks messy.
Missing Labels
Always annotate your drawing with leader lines and labels.
