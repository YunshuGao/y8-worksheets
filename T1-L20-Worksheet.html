🟢 Support
  • You can read directly from your notes — that is perfectly fine
  • Focus on 3 key points: restaurant name, one feature, one thing you learned
  • Hold up your model and point to the features as you speak
🟣 Extension
  • Present without reading directly from notes — use them as prompts only
  • Explain how your design considers sustainability, food safety, and customer experience
  • Make eye contact with the audience and speak clearly at a steady pace
  • Invite one question from the audience at the end of your presentation
