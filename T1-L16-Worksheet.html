Adding interior equipment, exterior details and documenting the construction process

Learning Intention: We are learning to add interior features, equipment, and exterior details to complete our 1:20 scale container restaurant model.
? DO NOW — Equipment List

Look at your floor plan from L14. List ALL the equipment pieces you need to build for the interior.

🛠 HOW TO BUILD SCALE EQUIPMENT
Bench / Counter
Rectangle of thick card — fold strip for legs underneath
Grill / Cooktop
Flat card on legs — draw 4 burner circles with marker
Sink
Small box shape — line with tinfoil for the basin
Refrigerator
Fold a small net into a box — like a mini container
Serving Counter
Long strip of card — fold into L-shape along window
Shelving
Stack small rectangles with strip supports between
Positioning tip: Place equipment in the SAME positions as your hand-drawn floor plan. Follow the workflow: Receive (near door) → Store (fridge) → Prepare (bench) → Cook (grill) → Serve (window). Leave at least 3 cm between items.
