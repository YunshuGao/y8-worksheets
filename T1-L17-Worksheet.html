📋 EVALUATION CRITERIA
What is evaluation? Evaluation means judging how well your design meets the original brief. You will assess your container restaurant against four criteria: appearance, functionality, sustainability, and safety. For each criterion, you need to explain what you did well AND what could be improved.
1. Appearance (Aesthetics)
How visually appealing is your design? Does it look professional and attract customers? Consider colour, signage, branding and overall visual quality.
2. Functionality
Does your restaurant work as a real food business? Is there a logical workflow from storage to cooking to serving? Can staff move safely around the space?
3. Sustainability
Does your design consider the environment? Have you reused a shipping container, included energy-efficient features, or minimised waste in your materials?
4. Safety
Is your restaurant safe for workers and customers? Are hot surfaces away from walkways? Is there ventilation, handwashing, and separate food preparation areas?

✍ EVALUATION WRITING — Claim, Evidence, Explanation (WB p. 39)
How to write an evaluation: For each criterion, write a Claim (what you believe about your design), provide Evidence (specific features or details from your model), give an Explanation (why this matters), and identify a Weakness / Improvement (what you would change next time).

Criterion 1 — Appearance (Aesthetics)

Claim — How well does your design look overall?
Evidence — What specific features make it visually appealing?
Explanation — Why do these features matter for a food business?
Weakness / Improvement — What would you change to improve the appearance?

Criterion 2 — Functionality

Claim — How well does your restaurant function as a working food business?
Evidence — What features support the workflow (store, prepare, cook, serve)?
Explanation — Why is workflow important in a food business?
Weakness / Improvement — What would you change to improve functionality?

Criterion 3 — Sustainability

Claim — How sustainable is your container restaurant design?
Evidence — What sustainable features did you include?
Explanation — Why does sustainability matter for a portable restaurant?
Weakness / Improvement — What sustainable feature could you add?

Criterion 4 — Safety

Claim — How safe is your container restaurant for workers and customers?
Evidence — What safety features are included in your design?
Explanation — Why are these safety features essential for a food business?
Weakness / Improvement — What safety feature could you add or improve?
