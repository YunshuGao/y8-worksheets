🏗 EXTERNAL DESIGN — 6 Required Features
1. Serving Window
Customer access point, typically 1.5 m wide. Positioned for easy ordering.
2. Counter/Bench
Serving and display area. Extends from the window for customer use.
3. Entry Door
Staff access. Opens outward for fire safety compliance.
4. Roof/Awning
Weather protection for customers. Extends 1–2 m from the container wall.
5. Ventilation System
Exhaust fans and grilles for air circulation and smoke extraction.
6. Signage Area
Branding, menu display and lighting. Visible from a distance.

ConventionHow to Use It
Leader linesThin lines with arrows pointing to features; text is written horizontally at the other end

Pencil gradesHB for guidelines, 2B/4B for outlines, 2H for construction lines
Line weightThick lines for outlines, thin lines for details and dimensions

🏠 INTERNAL FLOOR PLAN — 5 Required Areas
1. Cooking Zone
Grill, hotplate, fryer — positioned for exhaust extraction
2. Preparation Benches
Cutting boards, assembly area — between storage and cooking
3. Storage
Fridge (<5°C), freezer, dry goods — at rear of container
4. Wash-up
Dish sink + SEPARATE hand wash basin — near cooking zone
5. Serving Counter
Service area at serving window — at front of container
Key constraint: Your container is only 2.44 m wide. Zones must be arranged in a linear layout along the 6.1 m length. Minimum aisle width: 900 mm.
