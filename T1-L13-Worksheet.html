💻 DIGITAL TOOLS — Your Options

Floorplanner.com

  • Drag-and-drop room builder
  • Furniture library with kitchen items
  • Set exact room dimensions
  • Free for 1 project
  • Export as image or PDF

Canva (Floor Plan)

  • Search “floor plan” templates
  • Use shapes for walls and equipment
  • Easy text labels and colour
  • Free with school account
  • Download as PNG or PDF

🔁 KITCHEN WORKFLOW
Workflow = the logical order in which food moves through the kitchen. Good workflow prevents contamination and saves effort.

Receive (door) → Store (fridge/shelves) → Prepare (bench) → Cook (grill) → Serve (window)
